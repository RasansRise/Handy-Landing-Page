Handy Paper

Our first factory, located in Borg Al-Arab, Alexandria, is home to a vintage machine called ‘Recard’ from 1989; a tissue paper mill and transferred all its activities there. Originally, it primarily produced jumbo rolls. After undergoing renovations, ‘Recard’ now plays a crucial role in recycling, transforming highly selected economy waste paper ( Mix virgin pulp and waste paper ), contributing to our commitment to environmental sustainability. But it still is producing Jumbo rolls to this minute with capacity up to 15 tons/day - please add the details for the new machine. 03

Wet Wipes

Since its establishment in 2018, our wet wipes plant has been committed to producing high-quality products that meet both local and global standards. We prioritize customer satisfaction, which has earned us positive feedback from consumers worldwide, as well as full compliance with international cosmetic regulations.


Production Environment and Machinery

The entire production facility is designed with strict adherence to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). This includes specialized areas such as the production floor, laboratories, and warehouse, all built to uphold the highest hygiene standards. The plant is equipped with a large machine capable of handling 40 rolls, allowing for the production of both large and small-sized wipes. The flexibility of our machinery enables us to manufacture wipes ranging from 20 to 120 sheets, with a variety of packaging options, lid cap shapes, and film materials to suit diverse customer preferences. Our plant boasts one of the highest productivity rates in the Middle East, with the capacity to produce up to 3,000 cartons per day. To ensure consistent quality, the machine is outfitted with a metal detector and weigh-check system that automatically inspects and rejects products that fall outside of specification.


Water and Chemical Systems

Our production process incorporates an automated water purification system, which generates water of the highest quality, meeting the standards of the European Pharmacopoeia and WHO regulations. Additionally, the preparation, mixing, and injection of the chemical solution into the wipes are carried out with minimal human interaction, reducing the risk of impurities and microbial contamination.


Product Range

Our wet wipes are divided into four main categories, catering to a wide variety of customer needs:

  • Baby Wipes : Available in sizes of 20, 40, and 72 wipes.
  • Refreshment Multipurpose Wipes : Offered in sizes of 20, 40, 72, and 120 wipes.
  • Makeup Remover Wipes : Available in a 20-wipe size.
  • Antibacterial Disinfectant Wipes : Offered in a 40-wipe size.

Each product size features a unique fragrance and is available in three distinct packaging designs, offering customers a wide selection of 36 different design options.

Additionally, we offer premium-quality wipes for special customers in the 72 and 120 wipe sizes. All fragrances used are top-tier quality, hypoallergenic, and tested to ensure they do not cause irritation or allergic reactions.
