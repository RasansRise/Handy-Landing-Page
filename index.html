Sectors.

01

Handy Paper

Our first factory, located in New Borg El-Arab City, Alexandria, is home to a vintage machine called ‘Recard’ from 1989; a tissue paper mill and transferred all its activities there. Originally, it primarily produces jumbo rolls with capacity up to 15 tons/day. In 2016, we expanded with a new development for a new virgin pulp machinery from an Italian Company “ RECARD” with a capacity of approximately 80 tons per day. After undergoing renovations, ‘Recard’ now plays a crucial role in recycling, transforming highly selected economy waste paper ( Mix virgin pulp and waste paper ), contributing to our commitment to environmental sustainability. But it still is producing Jumbo rolls to this minute. This facility is catering to markets across KSA, Lebanon, Greece, Cyprus, Kuwait, Morocco, Syria, Sudan, Kenya, the Caribbean Sea region, and West Africa

02

Handy FMCG

In 2018, we expanded with a second facility in 6th of October City, featuring advanced South Korean technology and highly automated packaging machinery. This new Converting Plant is dedicated to producing a wide range of tissue paper products, including facial tissues, toilet paper, kitchen towels, table napkins, handkerchiefs, and C-fold towels.

03

Wet Wipes

Since its establishment in 2018, our wet wipes plant has been committed to producing high-quality products that meet both local and global standards. We prioritize customer satisfaction, which has earned us positive feedback from consumers worldwide, as well as full compliance with international cosmetic regulations.


Production Environment and Machinery

The entire production facility is designed with strict adherence to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). This includes specialized areas such as the production floor, laboratories, and warehouse, all built to uphold the highest hygiene standards. The plant is equipped with a large machine capable of handling 40 rolls, allowing for the production of both large and small-sized wipes. The flexibility of our machinery enables us to manufacture wipes ranging from 20 to 120 sheets, with a variety of packaging options, lid cap shapes, and film materials to suit diverse customer preferences. Our plant boasts one of the highest productivity rates in the Middle East, with the capacity to produce up to 3,000 cartons per day. To ensure consistent quality, the machine is outfitted with a metal detector and weigh-check system that automatically inspects and rejects products that fall outside of specification.


Water and Chemical Systems

Our production process incorporates an automated water purification system, which generates water of the highest quality, meeting the standards of the European Pharmacopoeia and WHO regulations.

Sustainability.

At Handy, we place sustainability at the heart of our work, prioritizing responsible sourcing, efficient production, and eco-friendly packaging to ensure minimal impact on the environment. Since our founding, we have looked beyond commercial expectations, aiming to create lasting value for society, nature, and humanity. Our commitment includes using Recard to recycle high-quality waste paper, combining virgin pulp with selected recycled materials to reduce waste and promote circularity. Guided by the United Nations Global Compact and Sustainable Development Goals, we align all our activities with these principles, driven by a shared mission to leave a better world for future generations. Through these ongoing efforts, we are proud to contribute positively to both the environment and the communities we serve.
